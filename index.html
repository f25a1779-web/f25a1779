Mathematical Formulations

Cumulative Yield Function \(f(x)\): $$f(x) = 320 \cdot e^{-8.5 \cdot e^{-0.18 \cdot x}}$$
Production Rate Derivative \(f'(x)\): $$f'(x) = f(x) \cdot (8.5 \cdot 0.18 \cdot e^{-0.18 \cdot x})$$
